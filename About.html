About

Hi, my name is Lawrence Ngo. I'm a graduate of University of California - San Diego with a degree in Cognitive Science, specialized in Human-Computer Interaction. With this degree, I am pursuing the career of UX/UI Design. I enjoy the process of human-centered design and it follows my lifelong passion of creating and wanting to help others. With UX/UI Design, I can help create local or global real-world solutions that will be useful.


I enjoy playing video games, watching movies and shows (especially anime), creating digital art, playing table tennis, frisbee, rock climbing, and just spending time with friends and family.
